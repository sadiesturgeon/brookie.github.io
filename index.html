A Local Dining Experience

Brookie provides a personal dining experience that intertwines the verdant Driftless surroundings with a warm, contemporary space overlooking the undulating hills of Mt. Horeb. Our fixed menu changes nightly, and evokes the joy of celebrating local food at the peak of its season. Let our four course meal guide you on a unique story crafted by our local, organic farmers and gatherers, passionate chefs, and dedicated servers.

An experience at Brookie is available by reservation only.
